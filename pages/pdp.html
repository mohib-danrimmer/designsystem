SMD Electrolytic Capacitors 100µF 50V are high-reliability aluminium electrolytic capacitors designed for surface-mount applications in industrial electronics, power supplies, and consumer equipment. Manufactured to IEC 60384-4 standards with a 2,000-hour lifetime rating at 105°C.

Supplied on a reel of 5,000 units, these capacitors meet RoHS compliance requirements and are suitable for automated PCB assembly. The low ESR characteristics make them ideal for high-frequency filtering applications in SMPS, inverters, and motor drives.
