Contextual information overlays for icon buttons, abbreviated labels, and complex field descriptions.

Designer Note Tooltips appear on hover after 300ms delay. Use top position by default, switch to bottom near viewport edge. Keep tooltip copy under 60 characters — for longer content use a Popover. Dark variant is default; light variant for use on dark backgrounds.
